I am a Geomatics Engineering researcher at the [Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad](https://www.iitism.ac.in/), specializing in **multisource remote sensing, InSAR deformation monitoring, and machine learning** for resource exploration, geohazard assessment, and mining-environment monitoring.

My work integrates SAR time-series analysis (PSInSAR and SBAS), hyperspectral and optical imagery, thermal and nighttime-light data, spatial statistics, deep-learning segmentation, and generative diffusion models for data-scarce settings. My M.Tech thesis, supervised by [Prof. Dheeraj Kumar](https://www.iitism.ac.in/), developed the first integrated decadal restoration prioritization framework for the Jharia Coalfield — a ~370 km² landscape shaped by a century of mining and persistent underground coal-seam fires.

I am seeking doctoral research (Fall 2027) that advances Earth-observation and AI methods for **mineral and energy resource exploration** and **surface-deformation hazard monitoring**, with a continuing interest in the socio-environmental and community-level dimensions of mining.

Thanks for visiting.

## Research directions

**Geospatial AI for landslide susceptibility.** GIS-based zonation, ensemble machine learning (XGBoost, WSRF), and geo-conditional diffusion models for tabular data augmentation in the Sikkim Himalaya.

**Multi-temporal InSAR deformation monitoring.** Sentinel-1 PSInSAR and SBAS time-series to resolve 3D kinematics of slow-moving landslides in the Gangtok region, quantifying the monsoon-driven acceleration of active slopes.

**Mining-environment monitoring and restoration prioritization.** Multi-sensor fusion (Sentinel-1/2, Landsat-8/9, VIIRS) with fuzzy AHP and spatial autocorrelation to map where post-mining restoration is most urgent, and why.

**Hyperspectral mineral mapping.** EO-1 Hyperion workflows (FLAASH, PCA/MNF, Spectral Angle Mapper) for mapping hematite, pyrite, and quartz-associated minerals at the Bailadila iron ore mine.

## Highlights

**GCT-TabDDPM: Geo-Conditional Diffusion Model for Landslide Augmentation** *(Apr 2026 – present)*
Development of a Geo-Conditional Transformer coupled with a Tabular Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model under ten domain-specific geomechanical constraints, addressing chronic data scarcity in geohazard modeling for the Sikkim Himalaya. Manuscript in preparation for the *International Journal of Disaster Risk Reduction*.

**Multi-Temporal InSAR Landslide Monitoring, Gangtok** *(Mar 2026 – present)*
Full-year Sentinel-1A processing through the ISCE2/MintPy pipeline (SBAS + PSInSAR, ~10 million coherent pixels) resolves 3D kinematics of active slow-moving landslides. Time-series analysis shows a three- to five-fold acceleration during the June–September monsoon. Manuscript ready for submission.

**First-Authored Journal Publication** *(2026)*
Das, M., Gautam, G. K., Jain, S., Bhat, M. F., Mankar, A. K., & Koner, R. (2026). *A comparative analysis of AHP, FR, AHP–FR and LR models for landslide susceptibility mapping in Sikkim Himalaya, India.* **Earth Surface Processes and Landforms**, 51(2), e70257.

**Jharia Coalfield Restoration Framework** *(2026)*
First integrated decadal restoration prioritization framework for the Jharia Coalfield. Spatially validated Restoration Priority Index (Moran's I = 0.8831, ROC–AUC = 0.8355) identifies where restoration is most urgent, at 10-meter resolution. Manuscript submitted to *Science of Remote Sensing* (Elsevier).

**Book Chapter in Press with Springer** *(2025)*
